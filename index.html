South African Braai Culture

Why I Enjoy South African Braai Culture

Braai is a big part of my South African culture. It is not just about cooking food, but also a family and social tradition.
Family and friends usually get together on weekends and public holidays, and I enjoy these moments with my family where we help with the preparations.
We cook braai meat such as chicken and boerewors, along with spices, salads, and grilled bread. The fire is made using wood and matches.
We also enjoy soft drinks while everything is prepared. I enjoy the conversation, soft music, laughter, and the relaxed atmosphere that brings everyone together.It feels special.

Popular Braai Dishes

  • Boerewors (traditional sausage)
  • Pap and sauce
  • Chicken pieces
  • Steak and lamb chops
  • Grilled bread and salads
